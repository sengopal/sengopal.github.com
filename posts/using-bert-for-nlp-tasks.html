Masked Language Modelling (MLM)

Fine Tune Training - Replace 15% of words in corpus with special [MASK] token and ask BERT to fill in the blank “__ at the light, to prevent a ticket.” - MLM task

Training - “Istanbul is a great [MASK] to visit”

Next Sentence Prediction (NSP)

  • Classification problem
  • Given two sentences, did sentence B come directly after sentence A?
  • True or False

A: “Istanbul is a great city to visit” B: “I was just there.” Did sentence B come directly after sentence A? Yes or No
